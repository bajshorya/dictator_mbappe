**2/**
How it works:
• Draft legends 1982–2022 (Position or By-Nation mode)
• Spend a star-points budget — no eleven 99s
• Build chemistry with same-nation / same-era links
• Pick a captain + tactics
• Then watch it all play out

**3/**
The sim is deep: 48 teams, seeded pots, fatigue & injuries that carry across rounds, red cards, extra time, rival manager styles — and **penalties you take yourself**.
Win it and you get a shareable champion card. There’s a daily challenge too.

**4/**

### Tagging strategy
1. **Main tweet:** tag at most **1–2** (e.g. `@433` *or* a FUT account). Let the content/video carry it.
2. **Reply to your own tweet** with: "built with @nextjs, open source 👉 repo" and tag the dev accounts there.
3. **Quote-tweet** live World Cup hype with "made a game for this 👇" — rides the trend.
4. **Reply** (don't just tag) to a creator's World Cup post with your 15s clip — far higher hit rate.
5. **DM** 5–10 smaller creators (10k–200k) a clip + one line: "made this for the World Cup, would you share it?" Small accounts share more.
6. **Timing:** post right at a match kickoff — football twitter peaks then.
